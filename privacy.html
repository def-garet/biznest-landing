Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

BIZNest is a digital marketplace designed to bridge buyers with local small and medium enterprises (SMEs). We provide a platform for product discovery, AR product viewing, order management, and direct communication. We are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding your data.

Data Collection and Usage

We strictly minimize data collection. Information entered into your user profile or Seller Dashboard—such as shipping addresses, purchase history, and product catalogs—is stored securely. This data is utilized solely to process marketplace orders, issue BIZNest Coins and Vouchers, and manage your account. We never sell your personal data to external third parties.

Device Permissions (AR & Media)

To deliver our core features, BIZNest requests specific device permissions. Camera access is strictly required to enable the AR Live View functionality and for uploading shop/profile images. BIZNest does not record or transmit ambient environmental data; media captures remain confined to their intended marketplace function.

Communications and Chat

Messages routed through the BIZNest AI Chatbot or direct Seller-to-Buyer communication channels are processed to facilitate customer service and order fulfillment. These channels are secured to prevent unauthorized interception.

Account Security & Deletion

Your BIZNest Wallet, Coin balance, and account details are protected utilizing industry-standard encryption. Users can request the complete deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team via the email provided below.
